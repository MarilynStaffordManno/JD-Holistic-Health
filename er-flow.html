Single Session · Gentle Entry

A gentle exhale for an overstimulated system.

When you're navigating chronic inflammation, your nervous system can get stuck in a tired-but-wired state. The Elemental Rhythm Flow journey is designed to help you break that cycle—dropping out of the thinking mind and settling into the body.

Investment $70–$130 sliding scale
Duration 75–90 minutes
Format Virtual · One-on-one
Availability Worldwide

What a Flow session is

Using rhythmic somatic movement, a curated musical soundscape, and a steady breath pattern, we create the internal safety required to calm the alarm of the nervous system. The pace is deliberately gentle. The point isn't catharsis. The point is restoration.

This is breathwork that meets you in the parasympathetic, not the sympathetic. We aren't trying to break anything open. We're letting things soften.

Who this is for

You feel overstimulated or stuck in a state of chronic stress. You want a gentler, more meditative entry point into somatic work—maybe you've been curious about breathwork but the more intense styles felt like too much. You're looking to support both your nervous system and your body's natural inflammatory response. You're a nutrition client who wants a practice that helps your body become more receptive to the work we're doing.

About the sliding scale

These sessions are offered on a sliding scale of $70–$130. Pay what feels right for your situation. There are no questions asked. I offer this because I believe somatic regulation should be accessible, not a luxury.

What's included

  • 75–90 minute virtual session
  • Curated music & rhythmic movement
  • Guided steady-breath pattern
  • Gentle integration time

What to expect

What the session feels like.

01

Settle in

We begin with a brief check-in and grounding. You'll get comfortable—blanket, pillow, low lighting. No performance required.

02

Flow

Forty-five minutes of guided breath, curated music, and rhythmic somatic movement. I hold the space and the pacing.

03

Land

We close with quiet integration time. Many clients describe a felt sense of softness they hadn't accessed in months.

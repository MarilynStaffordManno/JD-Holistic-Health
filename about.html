Meet Jennifer Stevens, CNP

My mission is to help you uncover underlying imbalances and give you the tools to finally thrive again.

My Story

My body was a puzzle I needed to solve.

For years, I knew something was off. I was constantly exhausted, always getting sick, and no one could tell me why. I also lived with a quiet frustration: my legs felt heavy and would swell, yet doctors checked my TSH and screened me for diabetes only to tell me everything was "normal."

In 2019, a "routine" cyst removal revealed carcinogenic thyroid tissue. I was only 37. I ate well and exercised, yet here I was. But deep down, I wasn't entirely surprised. The brain fog, bloating, chronic fatigue, and anxiety had been adding up for years.

Determined to take control, I worked with a holistic nutritionist who screened for underlying factors that traditional tests had missed: markers of parasites, gut dysbiosis, and high inflammation. With her guidance, I overhauled my lifestyle, restored balance to my gut, and supported my hormones. The transformation was so profound that I went back to school, became a Certified Nutritional Practitioner (CNP), and graduated valedictorian so I could help others find the answers that once felt out of reach.

In 2025, the final piece of my puzzle clicked into place with a Lipedema diagnosis. Finally, I had a name for the heaviness I had felt for decades. It was a moment of profound relief to finally be seen, but also the beginning of a deeper understanding of how lymphatic flow, thyroid health, and systemic inflammation are all interconnected.

Today, I've been cancer-free for over six years; and at 43, I feel better than I ever have. I work with people who feel stuck in their health journeys—struggling with fatigue, bloating, and inflammation—helping them identify root factors and reclaim their energy. I want to help you get there, too.

How I work

Slow medicine. Patient questions.

I don't believe in protocols handed down from on high. I believe in shared decision-making and informed, bio-individual care.

01

I listen first.

Before any protocol, before any test, I ask about your story. What hurts. What you've tried. What you're afraid will happen if nothing changes.

02

We look at the data.

Functional testing turns guesses into evidence. We use GI-MAP, DUTCH, and existing bloodwork to find the patterns conventional medicine overlooks.

03

We build a path together.

Nutrition, breathwork, supplementation, lifestyle. Always personalized. Always something you can actually do—and sustain.

A three-month partnership for lymphatic & immune support.

If you're living with Lipedema or an autoimmune challenge, you've likely been told there's nothing to do but watch and wait. I disagree. Over three months, we regulate your nervous system, refine your nutrition, and move from waiting into active, data-driven wellness.

Who this is for

This pathway is for the woman who has been told "there's nothing you can do" about her Lipedema, her autoimmune diagnosis, her chronic inflammation, or her recurrent flares—and refuses to accept that as a final answer. You're not looking for a miracle cure. You're looking for someone to walk with you, week after week, doing the slow, real work of regulation and repair.

Three months is the minimum I find meaningful for this kind of work. Bodies stuck in alarm don't reset in two weeks. But twelve weeks of consistent, layered support? That changes things.

How three months unfolds

We begin with a full 90-minute Deep-Dive Assessment to map every relevant thread—history, symptoms, stressors, goals. From there, we meet bi-weekly for sixty-minute strategy sessions. Some sessions focus on refining your nutritional protocol. Others move into progressive breathwork training, where I help you build capacity for somatic regulation. Many sessions weave both.

Between sessions, you have direct M–F messaging access through the Practice Better portal. Stuck in a flare-up at 10pm? Questions about a recipe? Confused by a supplement reaction? You message. I respond within the next business day. You're not navigating this alone.

The data layer

As your practitioner during these three months, you gain practitioner-level access to functional lab testing at my wholesale rates. If we agree GI-MAP, DUTCH, or another screening would clarify the path, you pay only what I pay. The same goes for practitioner-grade supplements—you get my professional discount.

What changes

Clients in this pathway typically report measurable shifts within the first 6–8 weeks: better digestion, more stable energy, reduced flare frequency, improved sleep, and a quieter nervous system. By the end of three months, most have built a sustainable rhythm they can carry forward without me—though many choose to continue with a maintenance cadence.

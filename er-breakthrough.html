How Breakthrough differs from Flow

Where a Flow session is gentle and parasympathetic, the Breakthrough is intentionally more intensive. The breath pattern is more active. The music is more dynamic. The arc is designed to mobilize stuck energy, not just soothe it.

This is the breathwork people are usually picturing when they hear the term—the kind that can produce significant emotional, somatic, or energetic shifts in a single session. It is, however, a lot. Which is why I recommend it primarily for those who already have some familiarity with breathwork.

Who this is for

You're already familiar with breathwork and want to go deeper. You feel stuck in a state of high stress or emotional plateau and you sense you need to move some things through. You want to experience the power of somatic breathwork without committing to a three-month container. You're a current nutrition client looking for a deep nervous-system reset between strategy sessions. You're curious about the Elemental Rhythm framework and want to experience its full impact.

What to expect after

Plan for a quiet evening following the session. Drink water. Don't make any major decisions for 24 hours. Many people sleep deeply that night. Many notice a softness in their bodies and a quiet in their minds that lasts for days. Some experience emotional waves in the 48 hours after—this is normal, and it's part of the integration.

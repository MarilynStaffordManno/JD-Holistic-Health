Frequently asked

Questions, gently answered.

How are sessions delivered?
All sessions are held virtually via the Practice Better portal—a HIPAA-compliant video platform. You'll receive a secure link before each appointment. I work with clients across North America and select international locations.
Do you accept insurance, HSA, or FSA?
I don't bill insurance directly. Many clients use HSA/FSA funds for nutrition consulting; please verify with your plan administrator. I provide superbills upon request for clients who wish to seek reimbursement.
What's the difference between a CNP and a registered dietitian?
A Certified Nutritional Practitioner (CNP) is a non-clinical wellness designation earned through the Institute of Holistic Nutrition. I focus on functional, root-cause approaches to nutrition. I'm not licensed to diagnose or treat medical conditions, and I always encourage working alongside your physician.
What happens on the free discovery call?
It's a 20-minute conversation—no pressure, no pitch. You share what you're navigating, what you've tried, and what you're hoping for. I'll ask a few questions, share how I work, and we'll decide together whether one of the pathways is the right fit. If not, I'll point you toward resources that may help.
How long until I see results?
Healing isn't linear, and bodies don't run on schedules. Most clients notice meaningful shifts within 4–8 weeks of consistent protocol work, with deeper change taking 3–6 months. The Signature Journey is structured around this timeline.
Can I work with you if I live outside the U.S.?
Yes—nutrition consulting, the Initial Deep-Dive Intake, and breathwork sessions are available to international clients. Functional lab testing through GI-MAP and DUTCH is currently limited to clients in North America (excluding NY, NJ, and RI due to state regulations).
Will I have to give up coffee, gluten, or wine?
My approach isn't about restriction for its own sake. Sometimes data shows that a temporary therapeutic protocol is wise; sometimes it doesn't. We'll work together to build something sustainable that respects your life, preferences, and culture.

Foundation Session

A place to land, and a plan to move forward.

A single 90-minute conversation where I review your full health journey, existing lab markers, and current symptoms—then send you home with a personalized nutritional protocol you can begin immediately.

Investment $250
Duration 90 minutes
Format Virtual · Zoom

Who this is for

This is for the woman who is tired of being told her labs are "normal" while feeling anything but. Maybe you've been bounced from specialist to specialist. Maybe you've done your own research, tried protocols you found online, and still aren't getting traction. Maybe you just want one practitioner to look at the whole picture, not just one slice of it.

The Deep-Dive Intake gives you 90 dedicated minutes—long enough to actually be heard, and short enough to leave with something tangible in hand.

What we cover

Together, we'll walk through your complete health history: childhood patterns, life events, dietary history, lifestyle stressors, sleep, digestion, energy, mood, hormones, and any existing diagnoses. Then we'll review whatever lab markers you bring—bloodwork from your physician, prior functional tests, or both.

I'll explain what those markers mean in plain language, where the patterns are, and what root factors I'd want to investigate further. By the end, you'll have an initial personalized nutritional protocol you can begin immediately—and clarity on whether deeper testing might be the next right step.

What you leave with

A written summary of our session, an initial protocol with specific food and lifestyle recommendations, and a clear sense of what to prioritize first. If a longer pathway makes sense (Gut-Rooted, Hormone Clarity, or the Signature Journey), the cost of this intake is fully credited toward it.

What's included

  • 90-minute virtual session
  • Comprehensive wellness assessment
  • Educational review of existing labs
  • Personalized nutritional protocol
  • Written summary & recommendations
  • Credit toward any pathway booked within 30 days

Why the gut, first?

The gut is a vital control center—not just for digestion, but for your immune system, your hormonal regulation, your mood, and your skin. When the microbiome is out of balance, almost nothing else in the body can fully heal. We see this pattern again and again: women come in with thyroid issues, inflammation, hormone dysregulation, autoimmunity. We work the gut, and the dominoes start falling in the right direction.

This pathway is the foundation. It's where I send most new clients whose symptoms point toward systemic inflammation, food sensitivities, fatigue, brain fog, or unexplained autoimmune patterns.

The GI-MAP test

The GI-MAP is a functional stool screening that uses DNA analysis to identify pathogens, opportunistic bacteria, parasites, fungal overgrowth, and digestive markers. It's far more comprehensive than the standard stool tests most physicians order—and unlike many at-home microbiome tests, it's clinically actionable.

The kit ships directly to your door. You collect at home, mail it back, and within about three weeks I receive a detailed report that we'll review together in a dedicated session.

Your phased protocol

Healing the gut isn't a one-and-done. We work in phases: first, we calm the system and remove inflammatory triggers. Then we work on what your specific results reveal—whether that's rebalancing flora, supporting digestion, or addressing pathogens with targeted nutrition and practitioner-grade supplementation.

Phase 2 builds on Phase 1, layering in foods, practices, and supplements to reinforce what's working and strengthen any remaining weak spots.
